Our Foundation

About Our Approach Questions

Do I need to clean before you arrive?

No. Please don’t feel pressure to pre-clean. We meet homes as they are.

Do you work with overwhelmed households?

Yes. Many clients call during demanding seasons, transitions, or periods of overwhelm.

What areas do you serve?

San Lorenzo Valley and Santa Cruz County, including Ben Lomond, Felton, Boulder Creek, Scotts Valley, and Santa Cruz.

Can I start with a one-time service?

Yes. Many clients begin with deep cleaning, then move into recurring support.
