About In The Details Cleaning Services

In The Details Cleaning Services is built around compassion, patience, lived experience, and care. Kelly brings a calm, respectful approach that helps people feel supported, not judged. Her background in the mental health field informs the way she communicates and supports clients through sensitive moments.

You do not need to clean before she arrives, and you do not need to apologize for where things are. The goal is always the same: a home that feels calmer, cleaner, and easier to manage.
