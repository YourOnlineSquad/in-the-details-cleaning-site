Why Specialized

Construction Dust Needs a Different Approach

Renovation residue moves differently than everyday dust. It settles in trim lines, corners, and finishing details that standard cleaning often misses.

We use a methodical room-by-room process to clear fine particulate and restore comfort without rushing.

Requested extras can include cabinet interior/exterior wipe-downs and targeted detail zones.
