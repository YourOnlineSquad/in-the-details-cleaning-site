FAQ

Post Construction Cleaning Questions

Do you remove construction debris?

No. Heavy debris, nails, large scraps, and contractor waste should be removed before our cleaning visit.

Can you clean fine dust after a remodel?

Yes. We focus on fine dust on reachable home surfaces, trim, fixtures, and floors based on the agreed scope.

Should contractors be finished first?

Yes. The best time to clean is after active work and dust producing tasks are complete.

Do you clean newly finished floors?

We can vacuum and mop finished floors with care. Product choice depends on flooring type and your instructions.

Do you serve remodel cleanups in Santa Cruz County?

Yes. We provide post project cleaning in San Lorenzo Valley and throughout Santa Cruz County.

Will one visit remove all dust forever?

Fine dust can resettle after cleaning. We reduce visible dust and can discuss follow up care if needed.
